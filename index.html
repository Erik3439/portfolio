About me

Hi there! My name is Erik John, a web designer and a front-end web developer. I have a passion for creating beautiful, functional websites that help businesses and organizations reach their goals. I have been designing, building, and maintaining websites for over 2 years. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js. I also have experience with web servers, databases, and content management systems.

I am passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in front-end development. Web Development

User Interface(UX Design)

I create products that provide a seamless and enjoyable user experience. The process involves research, prototyping, testing, and refining of user interfaces to ensure they are intuitive and easy to use. Creating/Maintaining Web Content
